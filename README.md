# 🏥 Hospital Management Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
Developed an end-to-end Hospital Management Analytics Dashboard using **Python, SQL, Excel, and Power BI** to analyze hospital operations, financial performance, patient demographics, departmental efficiency, and patient satisfaction. The project transforms raw healthcare data into actionable insights through interactive dashboards that support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- SQL
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization
- Business Intelligence

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives
- Analyze hospital revenue and financial performance.
- Monitor department-wise patient volume and revenue.
- Evaluate patient satisfaction and length of stay.
- Understand demographic and insurance trends.
- Identify top-performing and underperforming departments.
- Generate business insights for hospital management.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Department Performance
- Neurology generated the highest revenue (**₹39.7M**).
- Neurology handled the highest patient volume (**489 patients**).
- Cardio generated the lowest revenue (**₹1.83M**).
- General Medicine recorded the longest average length of stay (**8.40 days**).
- Cardiology achieved the highest patient satisfaction rating (**3.15/5**).

### Revenue Analysis
- Total hospital revenue reached **₹168.90M**.
- Government Insurance contributed the highest revenue (**₹63.2M**).
- May recorded the highest monthly revenue (**₹17.6M**).
- Treatment costs accounted for the largest share of overall expenses.

### Patient Analysis
- Senior Citizens represented the largest patient segment (**1,257 patients**).
- Bhopal contributed the highest number of patients (**539 patients**).
- Male and Female patient distribution remained nearly balanced.

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation
The dataset was cleaned and transformed using **Python (Pandas)**:
- Removed duplicate records.

- Validated and cleaned inconsistent records.
- Prepared analysis-ready datasets for SQL and Power BI.
